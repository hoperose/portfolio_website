About

I am Qian Yu (于茜), a prospective designer studying in a Masters program with a focus in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at University of California, Berkeley. With a mixed background of HCI, Social Science and Communications, I bring a holistic approach to design from a humanistic perspective. Meanwhile, to understand the user group I design for, I immerse myself in design research to uncover their needs and behaviors. In addition to design, I love photography, traveling, and cooking.         Read More  

My Story

I was born in Quzhou, a city with charming scenery and profound cultural heritage in Zhejiang Province, China. When I was a little girl, driven by imagination, I was good at creating things with drawings and handicrafts. Meanwhile, with a deep curiosity about the world, I liked talking to my parents, friends, and relatives with a lot of “why” questions.
